Frequently Asked

Why was he called the "Assist King"?

Özil earned the moniker due to his unrivaled ability to provide final passes, leading the assist charts across Europe's top leagues consistently throughout his prime.

What clubs did he play for?

His illustrious career saw him represent top-tier clubs including Schalke 04, Werder Bremen, Real Madrid, Arsenal, Fenerbahçe, and İstanbul Başakşehir.

Is he still active?

No, Mesut Özil officially announced his retirement from professional football in March 2023.
